# AURA Recommendation Engine

## Investment Recommendation

**Condition:**
- Salary credited recently
- Emergency fund is sufficient
- Savings ratio > 20%

**Recommendation:**
Suggest investing surplus funds into SIPs, mutual funds, or other suitable investment products.

---

## Budget Alert

**Condition:**
- Monthly spending increases by more than 15%

**Recommendation:**
Notify the user and suggest reducing discretionary expenses.

---

## Goal Acceleration

**Condition:**
- User is behind schedule on a financial goal

**Recommendation:**
Suggest increasing monthly savings or adjusting the goal timeline.

---

## FD Maturity

**Condition:**
- Fixed Deposit is nearing maturity

**Recommendation:**
Recommend reinvestment or redirecting funds toward an active financial goal.

---

## Bonus Allocation

**Condition:**
- Bonus or additional income is detected

**Recommendation:**
Allocate:
- 50% to Investments
- 30% to Financial Goals
- 20% to Lifestyle & Leisure

---

## Explainable AI

Every recommendation generated by AURA must include:

1. Observation
2. Reasoning
3. Recommendation
4. Expected Impact
5. Confidence Score
