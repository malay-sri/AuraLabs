# AURA – UX Blueprint

## Design Philosophy

AURA is designed as a proactive AI Relationship Banker rather than a traditional banking application. The experience focuses on helping users make better financial decisions through timely, personalized guidance.

The interface should feel modern, premium, intuitive, and trustworthy while minimizing cognitive load.

# Screen Structure

## 1. Splash Screen

Purpose:
Create a premium first impression.

Components:
- AURA Logo
- Tagline
- Animated Gradient Background
- Get Started Button

---

## 2. Login

Components:
- Secure Login
- Biometric Login (Prototype)
- Continue Button

---

## 3. Today (Home)

Purpose:
Give users an instant understanding of their financial position.

Sections:

- Greeting
- Financial Health Score
- Net Worth
- Monthly Savings
- AI Insight of the Day
- Quick Actions
- Recent Financial Activity

---

## 4. Talk to AURA

Purpose:
Voice-first AI financial assistant.

Features:
- Voice Input
- Voice Output
- Text Conversation
- Suggested Questions
- Conversation History

---

## 5. Future Simulator

Purpose:
Allow users to understand how financial decisions affect long-term goals.

Interactive Inputs:
- Salary
- Monthly Expenses
- Monthly Investment
- One-Time Purchase
- Loan Amount

Outputs:
- Goal Timeline
- Financial Health
- Savings Projection
- Net Worth Projection

---

## 6. Goals

Purpose:
Track financial goals.

Example Goals:
- Home
- Car
- Vacation
- Retirement

Each Goal Displays:
- Progress
- Timeline
- Monthly Recommendation
- AI Suggestion

---

## 7. Opportunities

Purpose:
Display proactive financial recommendations.

Examples:
- Increase SIP
- Reinvest FD
- Build Emergency Fund
- Reduce Lifestyle Spending

---

## 8. Profile

Components:
- Personal Information
- Connected Accounts
- AI Preferences
- Notifications
- Privacy Settings
