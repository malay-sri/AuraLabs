# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name
AURA – AI Relationship Banker

## Product Overview
AURA is an AI-powered, voice-first digital wealth management assistant integrated into the IDBI banking ecosystem. It delivers proactive, personalized financial guidance by understanding customer spending behavior, investment patterns, financial goals, and life events.

---

## Problem Statement

Customers often struggle to make informed financial decisions because existing banking applications primarily provide financial information rather than actionable guidance.

AURA addresses this gap by acting as an intelligent AI Relationship Banker that proactively recommends financial actions based on customer behavior and upcoming financial events.

---

## Target Users

- Young salaried professionals (24–40 years)
- First-time investors
- Retail banking customers
- Goal-oriented savers

---

## Objectives

- Deliver personalized wealth advisory.
- Increase customer financial awareness.
- Improve customer engagement.
- Encourage disciplined saving and investing.
- Make premium financial guidance accessible to all.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Voice AI Relationship Banker
Natural voice conversations with an AI avatar.

### 2. Financial Wellness Dashboard
Displays financial health, savings, investments, and personalized insights.

### 3. Life Events Intelligence
Identifies important financial events and provides proactive recommendations.

### 4. Future Simulator
Shows how financial decisions impact future goals.

### 5. Goal Planner
Tracks and recommends strategies for achieving financial goals.

### 6. Opportunity Feed
Provides personalized savings and investment opportunities.
